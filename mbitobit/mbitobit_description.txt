TITLE
      'MBITOBIT': module to fit bivariate Tobit regression

DESCRIPTION/AUTHOR(S)
      
      mbitobit fits a maximum-likelihood two-equation tobit models.
      It contains modules for easy estimation of predicted values (predict)
      and marginal effects (margins) for most commom outcomes for the model.
      It can also be used to obtained simulated data based on a previously estimated model.
      
      KW: Tobit
      KW: bivariate
      KW: margins 
      KW: predict
      KW: simulation
      
      Requires: Stata version 13
