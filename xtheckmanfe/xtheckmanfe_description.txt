TITLE
      'xtheckmanfe': module to fit Panel data models in the presence of endogeneity and selection

DESCRIPTION/AUTHOR(S)
      
      xtheckmanfe estimates fixed effects panel models in the presence of endogeneity and sample selection
using a Mundlak estimation device, following the estimator proposed in Wooldridge(1995), and 
Semykina and Wooldridge (2010). 
      xtheckmanfe provides standard errors using a bootstrap procedure.
